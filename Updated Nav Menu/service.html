Our Services

In-Home Care

Our in-home care service provides personalized support for elderly individuals who prefer to live in the comfort of their own homes. We offer assistance with daily tasks to help them maintain independence while ensuring safety and well-being.

Included Services

  • Assistance with eating and meal preparation
  • Showering, dressing, and personal hygiene support
  • Tidying and light housekeeping
  • Medication reminders
  • Scheduling and coordination of carer visits
  • Health and safety checks
  • Family communication and updates

Residential Care

Our residential care facilities offer 24/7 professional support for elderly individuals who require continuous care. With trained staff and a safe, comfortable environment, we ensure our residents receive compassionate assistance at all times.

Included Services

  • Full-time personal care (eating, showering, dressing)
  • On-site accommodation and room management
  • Access to utilities and facility amenities
  • Regular housekeeping and laundry services
  • Recreational and social activities
  • Medication administration and health monitoring
  • Meal services tailored to dietary needs
  • Inventory-provided necessities (e.g., toiletries, medication, food)

Medical Checkup

Regular medical checkups ensure early detection of health issues and support ongoing well-being. Our professional care team conducts thorough health assessments and monitors vital signs and medication compliance.

Included Services

  • Routine health monitoring and wellness checks
  • Medication tracking and administration
  • Coordination with healthcare professionals
  • Emergency response support
  • Updates to care plans based on health needs
  • Medical record keeping and family notifications
