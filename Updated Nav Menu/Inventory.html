How to Request Equipment

To request any of the items listed in our inventory:

  1. Speak with your assigned care coordinator or facility staff
  2. Fill out an equipment request form at the front desk
  3. Call our equipment services team at (03) 9876-5432
  4. Email your request to equipment@agecarefacility.com.au

Most standard equipment can be provided within 24 hours. Specialized items may require additional time. We offer both short-term loans and long-term arrangements depending on your needs.

Custom Equipment Requests

Don't see what you need? We can source specialized equipment upon request. Our team works with various suppliers to ensure we can accommodate specific needs. Please allow 3-5 business days for special order requests.
